OpenMapTiles comes with a variety of different styles optimized for the OpenMapTiles vector tile schema. The styles and schema are free and open-source and you can adapt the design and code for your project or commercial product however you like.

Our styles are written in the Mapbox GL style specification and you can edit them using Maputnik. You can either use them directly as basemap or as starting point for your own map style.
