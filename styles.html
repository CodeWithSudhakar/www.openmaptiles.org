What are map styles?

[Too formal definition] Styles includes symbology definition of items in the map. They are based on zoomlevels so it's dynamicly changes during you browse a map. Styles defines the parameters of the symbology in the maps such as color, ligthness, pattern etc.

In what format are they made (GL JSON)

How can you edit the styles? -> Docs

100% free, you can adjust the design for your project (even for a commercial product) and host them yourself.

Ready to use styles for global maps, or maps of countries

The listed styles are legally usable in any project, even commercial one. They can serve as the start for designing your own branded maps too.

Positron Star

Positron is beautiful light base map which is ideal for a non obtrusive basemap for your data visualizations. The cartography by Stamen Design is licensed under CC0.

Dark Matter Star

Dark Matter is a dark base map and a good starting point for other darker designs. The cartography by Stamen Design is licensed under CC0.

OSM Bright Star

OSM Bright is a general purpose base map showcasing the great detail of OpenStreetMap data. It is a good starting point for your own complex base maps.

Klokantech Basic Star

Klokantech Basic is basemap which tries to stay in the background and only give the most relevant information.
