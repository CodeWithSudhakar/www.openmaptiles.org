What is tile schema? How it is used. Designers editors

Open, well documented - free to hack, free to fork, free to implement in another software. Go and use it! no restrictions on commercial re-use (license BSD+CC-BY - please mention link to OpenMapTiles.org in documentation)

Developed by Klokan Technologies GmbH and Lukas Martinelli originally to implement the cartography of the Carto (CartoDB) Basemaps Positron.

The tile schema refined and improved in cooperation with Wikipedia foundation who hired Paul Norman to give us feedback, and many other contributors, Imresamu (quickstart), StiringHalo (3D buildings).
